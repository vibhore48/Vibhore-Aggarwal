Diecast Cars Company Sales Dashboard

Problem: Diecast Cars Company unable to manage & analyze sales data.

Solution: Designed dashboards and report to visualize useful data for actionable insights as follows:

    • Created Sales dashboard and projected there is 93% sales growth in 3rd Quarter.
    • Customer detail report highlighted top 25 customers driving 50% of total revenue.
    • Order detail highlights the top comments to why they were cancelled or on-hold.
    • Employee detail report shows which employee has garnered the most profit.
